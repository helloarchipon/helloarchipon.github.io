Bored Of The Hot Tub? Get A Pool By Vastu Shilpa

08 Jul, 2020 Â· Archipon

Getting bored of that inflatable hot tub? Or your toddler's bacteria laiden paddling pool? Why not treat yourself to an upgrade and get a pool. Fuck it. Why stop there. Lets go all out Mary. Lets build a spa.

That's exactly what these lucky ducks have done. And what a spa it is.

According to the designers the spa/pool is an underground extension to an existing residence. The property itself is located somewhere in Ahmedabad, India.

Being a semi-arid climate, I imagine the pool is a welcome addition. Somewhere to kick back and rela... oh, it's a lap-pool. Ahem. Somewhere to exhaust yourself at the end of a long hot day. And if that isn't enough, they've thrown in a gym for good measure.

We may be on lock-down Mary, but we're going to come out of this fitter than ever.

Still the pool/spa/gym is a thing of beauty. A cantilevered walkway creates a spot to dangle your legs over the pool, and stir into its blue-green depths.

Alternatively, you can choose to live dangerously and stare at your smart phone, as pictured.

Jokes aside, the space looks amazing and the excution is flawless. When looking at the floor plan, the other spaces appear quite conventional in size. Although it's hard to get a real feel for the place without more pictures.

Project by Vastu Shilpa Consultants. Lots of great things on their website.
